SnoutScan: Non-Invasive Biometric & AI System for Dog Care & Recovery

A unified, smartphone-accessible platform that uses dog nose-print recognition, GPS-based recovery, AI-driven ethical breeding guidance, and community cruelty detection to transform canine welfare management.

Project Overview

🔬

Research Area

AI-powered canine biometrics, deep learning-based nose-print recognition, lost-pet recovery, ethical breeding recommendation, and community-driven animal cruelty detection.

🎯

Objectives

Develop a non-invasive biometric identification system, a GPS-based lost-pet recovery workflow, an AI breeding recommendation engine, and a deep learning cruelty detection module — integrated in a single platform.

⚙️

Methodology

Agile-aligned, five-phase methodology covering requirements analysis, system & UI/UX design, RESTful API and ML model development, integration and usability testing, and cloud deployment.

Abstract

As dogs increasingly take on the role of family members globally, the consequences of pet misidentification, abandonment, and delayed recovery remain persistent and deeply concerning. Traditional identification methods — tags, collars, and microchips — are vulnerable to damage, loss, tampering, or require invasive procedures and specialised readers, with these shortcomings most severe in low-resource or high-mobility environments.

SnoutScan addresses this gap by proposing a comprehensive, AI-powered pet identification and welfare platform that uses biometric nose-print matching, GPS-based geolocation, visual similarity detection, and cruelty recognition. The system leverages CNNs, transformer-based architectures, and contrastive learning models to support multimodal functionality — including secure digital profiles, community alerts, and tamper-proof proof of ownership.

Beyond identification, the platform integrates an AI-driven ethical breeding recommendation engine that analyses genetic diversity and hereditary health risks, and a community cruelty detection module that classifies abuse indicators from user-submitted images and dispatches alerts to welfare authorities. This multifunctional ecosystem offers a privacy-conscious, scalable, and highly accurate alternative to invasive or error-prone identification methods, fostering responsible pet care at scale.

Research Components

🐾

Biometric Identification

Smartphone nose-print capture, CNN-based feature extraction, and biometric signature generation linked to a secure digital dog profile. Targets 98–99% verification accuracy.

📍

Lost & Found Recovery

Multi-modal image matching combined with real-time geolocation alerts to nearby users, shelters, and vets. Includes encrypted in-app communication for verified identity coordination.

🧬

Ethical Breeding AI

AI recommendation engine that analyses genetic diversity, health screenings, and breeding history to rank ethical pairing candidates and flag inbreeding risks.

🚨

Cruelty Detection

Deep learning object detection model that classifies abuse and neglect from community-submitted images, links findings to verified dog profiles, and dispatches alerts to welfare authorities.
