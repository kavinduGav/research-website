About This Project

SnoutScan is a final-year research project conducted as part of the IT4010 Research Project module at the Faculty of Computing, SLIIT (Academic Year 2025/26). The project is carried out under the supervision of Prof. Dasuni Nawinna and Mr. Jeewaka Perera.

The team of four developed an AI-powered, non-invasive biometric platform for dog identification and welfare management. Each member owns one of the four core components - biometric nose-print identification, lost and found recovery, ethical breeding recommendation, and community cruelty detection - contributing to a unified, privacy-preserving, and scalable canine welfare ecosystem.
